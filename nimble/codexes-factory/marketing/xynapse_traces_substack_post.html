The Vision: Ancient Practice, Modern Purpose

Pilsa (필사) is a traditional Korean practice of copying literary passages by hand to improve mindfulness and gain new perspectives. Our xynapse traces imprint offers a fresh take on this ancient tradition, pairing readers with thought-provoking content from the frontiers of human knowledge.

Each book in the xynapse traces collection is designed for transcriptive meditation — a contemplative practice where readers engage deeply with carefully curated quotations, key concepts, and essential insights by copying them by hand. This process transforms passive reading into active learning, fostering both understanding and mindfulness.
