About This Book

What if the very force that propels you to greatness also harbors the seeds of your destruction?Visionary Drive: Passion versus Peril delves into the exhilarating yet treacherous landscape of relentless ambition, revealing the delicate balance between groundbreaking innovation and the profound personal cost it often demands. This compelling book, a deep dive into leadership psychology and entrepreneurial passion, meticulously dissects the complex motivations behind those who "invent the future." From the "unconscious concern for excellence" that fuels a growth mindset to the "vaulting ambition" warned by Shakespeare, it explores the grit and perseverance essential for long-term goals. Yet, it unflinchingly examines the darker currents: the potential for narcissism, Machiavellian cunning, and hubris, drawing on insights from Dweck, Duckworth, Campbell, and timeless philosophical wisdom.
